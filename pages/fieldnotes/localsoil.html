EINDHOVEN, NETHERLANDS

ON LOCAL SOIL

with Atelier NL

9 / 13     2019 05 23     51.4416° N, 5.4697° E
23 °C W 19 KM/H

In Eindhoven, we spoke to Nadine Sterk and Lonny van Ryswyck who make up Atelier NL for our film on ceramics. Their specialization is in earthenware.

Their work is not a series of products or singular works, but a body of work — a profoundly deep, wide, and thorough investigation spanning over a decade. They passionately explore the untapped value, cultural as well as economic, in the ground beneath our feet.

Sand, soil, and clay. Atelier NL’s work is always focused where most people fail to look – and even if they did, they would likely miss the valuable qualities in those materials and resources.
