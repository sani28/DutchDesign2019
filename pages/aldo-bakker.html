Aldo Bakker

Aldo Bakker is continuing to add objects to his oeuvre. He always begins with a certain evocation, arriving to the form through expressive sketches and words to capture it without compromise. His objects feel at ease, while also existing in unexpected ways.

I get fascinated by certain elements of forms, they trigger me and I question them until they make sense to me and I understand what else they can become.

I want my things to be at ease. As long as a piece is quiet and confident, I do not want to interfere anymore.

There's always a moment when I bring the elements together, that's when the thing starts to speak for itself.

The most important is that an object becomes independent, otherwise it will kick you out of your dream.
