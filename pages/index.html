Team Legacy DutchDesign

In this complex age, we embrace ambiguity and contradiction. dutchDesign is an ongoing field school for filmmaking, research, and reportage. The protagonists are individuals who define their own worlds — who blur and destroy boundaries, definitions, and limitations. Through 16 interviews and 14 reflections, it attempts to make Dutch culture fleetingly visible.
