DutchDesign

We embrace ambiguity and contradiction. dutchDesign is an on-going platform for field research, observation, & reportage. The protagonists are individuals who shape and define their own worlds; working to blur and destroy boundaries, definitions, and limitations. It attempts to make Dutch design culture fleetingly visible while leaving space for it to describe itself.
