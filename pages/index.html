Team Legacy DutchDesign

In this complex age, we embrace ambiguity and contradiction. dutchDesign is an ongoing platform for field research, observation, and reportage. The protagonists are individuals who define their own worlds; who blur and destroy boundaries, definitions, and limitations. It attempts to make Dutch design culture fleetingly visible while leaving space for it to describe itself.
